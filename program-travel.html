Program Introduction

This program is designed for travelers and long stays in China. Lessons weave together practical topics—food, lodging, transport, shopping, emergencies—and the culture of your chosen destinations so you feel prepared for every moment on the road.

Tell us your current level, travel plans, and the experiences you want to unlock. We customize your curriculum and match you with a tutor who understands your route and interests.

Our learning-practice model blends targeted language instruction with immersive experiences. Offline classes take you into real-life scenarios—ordering local dishes, riding public transport, exploring landmarks—with your tutor by your side. Online sessions recreate the same contexts virtually, ensuring consistent quality wherever you are.

Program Highlights

  • Travel-focused topics covering dining, accommodation, transportation, shopping, healthcare, and safety.
  • Customized itineraries and cultural immersion aligned to your preferred destinations.
  • Practice in authentic settings with your tutor guiding every interaction.
  • Support from teaching and academic teams to fine-tune lessons as your journey unfolds.

Services & Activities Included (Offline)

Travel Chinese Instruction

Intensive 4/5/6 hour daily lessons (minimum one-week booking) with expert tutors focused on your travel objectives and destinations.

Airport Pick-up & Drop-off

Complimentary Kunming airport transfers. Share your arrival details and our team will welcome you. Weekend arrivals let you start fresh on Monday.

Meals Monday–Friday

Taste authentic Chinese and ethnic minority cuisine at every location. Conversations with locals during meals add extra practice and cultural exchange.

Free Coffee & Tea (Kunming HQ)

Enjoy native Yunnan tea and specialty coffee daily while studying at headquarters, immersing yourself in local beverage culture.

Accommodation

Private ensuite room with daily cleaning, 24-hour hot water, Wi-Fi, and all essentials—seven nights per week across all LensTalk locations.

Teaching Management

Teaching supervisors design your travel-focused learning plan, coach tutors, and ensure lesson quality aligns with your journey.

Academic Affairs Support

The academic director aligns tutor assignments with your progress, gathers feedback, and resolves issues quickly so your plans stay on track.

24/7 Lifestyle Support

We help you navigate local logistics, cultural differences, and any unexpected situations—anytime, anywhere, just like trusted friends.

Community Activities (Kunming HQ)

Join weekly Chinese TV show viewings, sports like basketball or badminton, and cultural workshops to deepen your understanding of local life.

Visa Support

Receive detailed visa guidance and supporting documents once your enrollment is confirmed to make travel planning easier.
