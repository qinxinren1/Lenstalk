Program Introduction

Designed for professionals and entrepreneurs, this program focuses on accurate expression in business settings, protocol mastery, and cross-cultural intelligence. It solves the common challenge of managing daily conversations in Chinese yet struggling to perform in negotiations, meetings, or presentations.

Share your current level, business goals, industry, and preferred communication scenarios. We will tailor a curriculum and connect you with a tutor experienced in your sector to ensure immediate workplace relevance.

Our learning-practice model combines targeted language lessons with hands-on simulations. Offline classes include site visits, client meetings, and negotiation practice where your tutor guides you live. Online learners receive the same high-impact role plays and situational training to build confidence wherever you are.
