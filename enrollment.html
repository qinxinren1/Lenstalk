Frequently Asked Questions

Applications are accepted year-round, but we recommend applying at least 3 months before your desired start date to allow time for visa processing and document verification.
No previous Chinese knowledge is required! We offer programs for complete beginners to advanced learners. Our placement test will determine your appropriate level.
Students need an X1 visa (long-term study) or X2 visa (short-term study). We provide all necessary documents and guidance for the visa application process.
International students on X1 visas can apply for part-time work permission after 6 months of study. We can assist with the application process.
Cancellation policies vary by timing. Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before start date, partial refund for 15-30 days, and no refund for less than 15 days notice.

How to Enroll

1

Application

Submit your online application with required documents. This includes your personal information, educational background, and program preferences.

2

Review

Our admissions team reviews your application within 3-5 business days. We'll verify your documents and check program availability.

3

Interview

Online interview to assess your goals, motivation, and current Chinese language level. This helps us recommend the best program for you.

4

Acceptance

Receive your acceptance letter and enrollment package via email. This includes program details, start dates, and next steps.

5

Visa Support

We assist with visa application process, including providing necessary documents and guidance for Chinese student visa (X1/X2).

6

Orientation

Welcome orientation and campus tour upon arrival. Meet your classmates, get your student ID, and learn about campus facilities.
