★ Program Introduction

Intensive learning program designed for all language level/goals and culture demand. Don't worry even if you are the complete beginners, our tutors will provide targeted teaching for students at different language stages. Three meals Monday to Friday and accommodation are included.

You only need to tell us your language level, language learning goals or cultural experience needs, we will customize your course and match the most suitable tutor for you.

We adopt a learning-practice combination model. Your tutor will not only help you to learn language, but also create real contexts and accompany you to practice in offline classes, together with culture activities.

Your tutor will also simulate real contexts in online classes and provide same quality instruction for you.

★ Schedule

Offline Classes

Off line classes are held from every Monday to Friday. You can choose to study 4 hours, 5 hours or 6 hours per day. The daily classes start at 9am every day and the schedule is very flexible, it can be adjusted according to your dynamic requirements.

(For example, if you choose to take 5 hours classes per day, we can have 3 hours in the morning and 2 hours in the afternoon, or 2 hours in the morning and 3 hours in the afternoon or even other combinations.)

The content and format of the classes can also be adjusted according to your specific needs.

Online Classes

The class time of online courses will be arranged according to your and your tutor's time schedule and it will also be flexible if you need to make any adjustments.
